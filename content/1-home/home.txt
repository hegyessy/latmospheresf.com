About: 

Ingrid Ternynck is the Founder and Principal of L’Atmosphere, LLC. Ingrid’s signature style brings a unique blend of European and American aesthetics derived from her design experience in Prague, Paris and San Francisco. Ingrid’s exquisitely curated style elegantly melds modern and traditional design. She aspires to strike the perfect balance between luxury and artistry with the personality and needs of her clients.

L’Atmosphere’s projects include all aspects of decorating from selecting tile and lighting to finding the perfect art, antiques and furnishings for your home. Whether you are contemplating a complete remodel or are simply refreshing a room, Ingrid brings two decades of experience to her clients. Her boutique approach allows her to be involved in every aspect of a project and provide the highest quality product to her clients.
